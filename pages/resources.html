Contact a Doctor

The first person you should go to is your physician. While it may be uncomfortable, they're there to help you and can provide the professional help needed.
Time with doctors is limited, so it's good to come prepared. Exporting data from here is a great way to help the doctor diagnose any problems effectively and come prepared to those meetings.
Remember, it's totally okay to get help for this. Mental health issues aren't something you can just get over. So, go get help and feel a lot better!
