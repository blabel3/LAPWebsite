We made this to help people.

We set out with the goal of helping teens in St. Louis manage depression and find real help. We learned a lot about depression along the way, both about depression and how many mental illnesses can be difficult to work with due to their nature. Because here are a lot of misconceptions about depression out there and how important it is for friends and family to be supportive and as understanding as possible, we added more sections onto Resources that let us share our research. Depression is a big issue, but it is preventable. It's our duty to work towards that, as all of us are affected and we all deserve the help.
